## Reporting a Vulnerability

1. Goto the [issues](https://github.com/iamBijoyKar/emojis-pixel-art/issues) section of this project 
2. Click `New Issue`
3. Click `Report a Vulnerability`
4. Then propose your issue in a descriptive way
